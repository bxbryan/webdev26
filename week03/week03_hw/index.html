Whiskey Sour

Instructions

  1. Add whiskey, lemon juice, simple syrup, and egg white to a shaker.
  2. Dry shake (so no ice) for 10â€“15 seconds to emulsify the egg white.
  3. Add ice and shake again until chilled through.
  4. Strain into chilled coupe or rocks glass.
  5. (Optional) Garnish with lemon twist or cherry.

Notes

  • For a lighter foam, shake longer during the dry shake.
  • The egg white is the soul to me. Please, make it nice and foamy with an egg white if possible.

Overview

A classic favorite.

Bright, refreshing, and easy to make at home.

Ingredients

Must-haves

  • 2 oz bourbon or rye whiskey
  • 3/4 oz fresh lemon juice
  • 3/4 oz simple syrup
  • 1 egg white
  • Ice

Optionals

  • Lemon twist
  • Cherry

Equipment

  • Cocktail shaker
  • Jigger
  • Strainer
  • Coupe or rocks glass
